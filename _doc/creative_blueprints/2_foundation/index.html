Why CleanFlow

The Problem

Great cleans lost to
poor operations.

Bookings fall through the cracks

Jobs managed across texts, emails, and spreadsheets. Double-bookings, missed slots, and no single source of truth.

Teams don't know where to go

Assignments via phone calls or group chats. No brief, no accountability, no visibility into who's available.

Quality problems surface too late

Client dissatisfaction discovered when they cancel — not after every visit when it could still be fixed.

The Solution

One platform. Full
operational clarity.

Visual scheduling calendar

Drag-and-drop job management. Every booking in one place — created, edited, and rescheduled in seconds.

Team dispatch with full context

Assign crews per job with availability awareness. Teams see their schedule, address, and briefing — nothing missed.

Automated feedback loop

Post-visit feedback requests sent automatically. Ratings and comments tracked per client so issues are caught early.

# Product Overview

## Product Name
CleanFlow

## One-line Description
Scheduling, team dispatch, and client feedback — one platform built for cleaning service owners who want to deliver consistent quality and grow their client base.

## Problem
Cleaning service owners juggle bookings across texts and spreadsheets, struggle to assign the right team to each job, and lose track of client satisfaction until it's too late to fix it. The friction kills repeat business and makes scaling nearly impossible.

## Solution
CleanFlow centralises everything: owners schedule cleanings on a visual calendar, assign field teams to each job, and automatically collect client feedback after every visit. Real-time visibility into team performance and client satisfaction turns daily operations into a growth engine.

## Primary Users
- **Cleaning business owners / operators** — need a bird's-eye view of all jobs, teams, and client health
- **Team leads / supervisors** — need clear job assignments, location, and briefing notes
- **Clients** (light touch) — submit feedback after service via a simple post-visit form

## Core Features
1. **Scheduling calendar** — drag-and-drop booking view; create, edit, and reschedule cleanings with a few clicks
2. **Team assignment** — assign one or more cleaning crews to each job; manage availability and workload
3. **Client feedback tracking** — automated post-visit feedback requests; track ratings, comments, and trends per client
4. **Quality dashboard** — aggregate view of team performance and client satisfaction scores over time
5. **Client management** — profiles with address, service history, feedback record, and notes
6. **Notification & reminders** — automated reminders to clients and teams before each appointment

## Positioning
Mid-market SaaS for small-to-medium cleaning businesses (1–50 employees). Priced as a simple monthly subscription. Positioned against generic scheduling tools (Calendly, Jobber, Housecall Pro) with a tighter focus on quality consistency and client retention — not just booking volume.

## Brand Tone
Professional, calm, trustworthy. The product feels organised and reliable — like a well-run business. Avoids startup hype. Clean aesthetics.

## Brand Colors
- **Primary**: Teal (#0d9488)
- **Secondary**: Slate (#334155)
- **Accent**: Emerald (#10b981)

## Strategic Principles
- Consistency first: every feature exists to help owners deliver a reliably excellent clean
- Minimal admin: automate the repetitive (reminders, feedback requests, assignment suggestions)
- Growth through retention: client feedback and satisfaction data are first-class — not an afterthought
